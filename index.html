The Story

Where levels decide worth,
falling behind means being left behind.

Shin advances more slowly than those around him. Slowly enough to be forgotten. Slowly enough to be abandoned.

Bound by a curse no one understands, every level is built on choices he never shares.

As ambition, betrayal, and unseen enemies gather in the shadows, he keeps moving forward — convinced he can carry the burden alone. That as long as he protects the people he cares for most, the truth doesn't matter.
